Background

Built inside the machine. Now working outside of it.

For 25 years, I led project management and business analytics initiatives at Fortune 50 organizations — the kind of environments where execution discipline, data integrity, and operational rigor aren't optional. They're survival.

I managed programs with nine-figure budgets, built analytics infrastructures from the ground up, and guided organizations through the kind of complex transformations that most companies attempt once and never fully recover from.

What I noticed, year after year, was that smaller companies — particularly in financial services and utilities — were fighting the same battles with a fraction of the resources. Smart leadership teams. Real ambition. But without the operational infrastructure to execute at the level they were capable of.

The why

The gap no big firm wants to fill.

Large consulting firms don't want your business if you're a $200M credit union or a regional utility with 50,000 customers. You're too small for their model. Too complex for a generalist freelancer. And too important to your community to settle for mediocre execution.

That's exactly where I work. Reimagining Consulting exists to give growing financial services firms and regional utilities access to the same caliber of project management and analytics thinking that Fortune 50 organizations have taken for granted for decades.

No junior associates doing the work. No bloated methodologies. Just direct, senior-level engagement — and a genuine commitment to results you can measure.
